Mac and Cheese

Ingredients:

  • maccoroni
  • cheese
  • milk
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps:

  1. Put maccoroni in a pan with milk and cheese in it
  2. add salt and pepper to taste
  3. put the pan in an oven for 15 minutes
  4. after 15 minutes remove the pan from oven and your mac nd cheese is ready
