Pizza

Discription:

a dish of italian origin, consisting of a flat round base of dough baked with a toppings of tomato and cheese.

Ingredients:

  • cheese
  • pizza dough
  • flour
  • tomato puree
  • mozzarella

Steps:

  1. stretch the pizza dough to a flat bread like shape with the help of flour and don't toss it
  2. top the dough off with tomato puree, cheese and mozzarella
  3. bake the pizza till it gets nicely brown or for 8 to 10 minutes
